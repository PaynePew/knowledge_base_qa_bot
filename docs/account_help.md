# Account Help

## Reset Password

Customers can reset their password from the sign-in page via "Forgot password". New passwords must be at least 10 characters and include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and digits. After 5 consecutive failed login attempts the account is locked for 30 minutes; completing a password reset unlocks it early.

## Two-Step Verification

Customers can enable two-step verification under Account Settings → Security, using either SMS one-time codes or an authenticator app. Once enabled, logins from new devices require the second factor.

## Delete Account

Customers can request account deletion from Account Settings. The request enters a 14-day grace period, after which the account is permanently deleted. Logging in during the grace period cancels the request.
