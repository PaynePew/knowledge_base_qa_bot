# Store Services Guide

## Store locations and pickup service

ACME Shop operates 3 physical stores: Taipei Xinyi, Taichung Xitun, and Kaohsiung Dream Mall. All three stores offer in-store pickup for online orders free of charge; please present your order barcode and a valid photo ID when collecting.

## Pickup window

Head to the store once the order status shows Ready for pickup (usually within 2 hours of payment); orders are held for **3 calendar days** from the notification, then cancelled automatically with a full refund.
