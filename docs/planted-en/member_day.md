# Member Day

## Member Day perks

On the monthly Member Day, Gold and Platinum members enjoy bonus-point promotions. Point earning rules are unchanged: every NT$100 spent earns 1 point, based on the amount actually paid.

## Member Day perks

During Member Day, point redemption follows the everyday rules: 1 point equals NT$1, and at most 50% of an order's total can be paid with points.
