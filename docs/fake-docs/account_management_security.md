# Account Management & Security

This guide is the central reference for setting up an account, maintaining passwords and credentials, configuring security controls, and deleting an account when it is no longer needed.

## Registration

Three sign-up methods are supported: **email**, **Google**, and **LINE**. If you register with a third-party account, you can still set a password under Account Settings as a backup login method.

## Password Rules

Passwords must be at least **10 characters** and include **uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and digits**.

## Two-Step Verification

Enable two-step verification under **Account Settings → Security**. Two methods are supported:

- SMS one-time codes
- Authenticator apps (TOTP)

Once enabled, every login from a new device requires the second factor.

## Login Protection

After **5 consecutive failed login attempts**, the account is locked for **30 minutes**. You can unlock early by resetting your password via "Forgot password".

## Account Deletion

Deletion requests submitted under Account Settings enter a **14-day grace period**, after which the account is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered. Logging in during the grace period cancels the request.
