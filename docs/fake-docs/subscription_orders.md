# Subscription Orders

## Setting Up a Subscription

To enroll a product in auto-replenishment with Acme Shop, start by logging into your account on our website. Once logged in, navigate to the product page of the item you wish to subscribe to. You will see an option labeled "Subscribe & Save" or "Auto-Replenishment" near the purchase button. Click on this option to begin setting up your subscription. Select your desired replenishment frequency, which can range from weekly to monthly intervals. Note that some products may offer specific frequency options based on their availability or nature, like seasonal items or those with shorter shelf lives. 

After selecting the frequency, you will be prompted to confirm your shipping details and payment method. Acme Shop accepts various payment options, including credit/debit cards and PayPal. Ensure that the payment method selected is valid and has sufficient funds to cover the future orders. Once confirmed, you will receive a confirmation email with the details of your subscription, including the next scheduled delivery date. If you wish to modify, pause, or cancel your subscription, you can do so at any time through the "My Subscriptions" section in your account. 

While there are no additional fees for setting up a subscription, some discounts may apply, providing savings compared to one-time purchases. Be aware that certain promotions or discount codes may not be applicable to subscription orders. In cases where a product is temporarily out of stock, you will be notified via email, and your subscription will automatically resume once the item is back in stock. If you encounter any issues or have specific questions, our customer support team is available to assist you 24/7 via chat or email.

## Managing a Subscription

To manage your subscription with Acme Shop, you have the flexibility to pause, skip, or cancel your subscription based on your needs. To pause your subscription, log in to your account and navigate to the "My Subscriptions" section. From there, select the subscription you wish to pause and click on the "Pause Subscription" option. You can choose to pause your subscription for up to three months. Please note that you must pause your subscription at least 24 hours before your next billing cycle to avoid being charged for the upcoming cycle.

If you wish to skip a delivery instead, follow similar steps by selecting "Skip Delivery" under your chosen subscription. This action will skip your next scheduled delivery but keep your subscription active for subsequent periods. Skipping a delivery is ideal if you have excess stock or will be unavailable to receive your package. Be sure to skip at least 48 hours before your scheduled delivery date to ensure your request is processed in time.

To cancel your subscription, access the "My Subscriptions" area and click on "Cancel Subscription." You will be asked to confirm your cancellation and provide a reason for ending your subscription. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before your next billing cycle to avoid being charged. If you have any remaining pre-paid deliveries, these will still be fulfilled unless you contact our customer support for a prorated refund, subject to our refund policy. Keep in mind that reactivating a canceled subscription may be subject to product availability and current pricing. If you have any questions or need further assistance, our customer support team is ready to help.

## Subscription Pricing Changes

At Acme Shop, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional value and transparent pricing. When subscription pricing changes, it can affect your active subscriptions in several ways. If there is a scheduled price increase for a product you are subscribed to, you will be notified via email at least 30 days in advance. This ensures you have ample time to decide whether to continue, modify, or cancel your subscription before the new pricing takes effect.

For existing subscriptions, the new pricing will be applied on the subsequent billing cycle following the effective date of the price change. If you have prepaid for a subscription, the new rate will only apply once your prepaid period has concluded. If a price decrease occurs, the reduced pricing will automatically be applied to your next billing cycle, ensuring you benefit from the savings without any additional action required on your part.

In cases where a pricing change is due to external factors, such as an increase in supplier costs or changes in market conditions, we strive to minimize any impact on our customers. If you have any questions or wish to discuss how a pricing change affects your specific subscription, our customer support team is available to assist you. Feel free to reach out to us via email or through our online chat service for personalized support.
