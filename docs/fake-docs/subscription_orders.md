# Subscription Orders

This document covers subscription eligibility, discounts, pausing, and cancellation.

## Eligible Items & Cycles

Subscriptions are available for **consumable products** (e.g. filters, cleaning supplies), with delivery cycles of **30, 60, or 90 days**.

## Subscription Benefits

- Each cycle is billed at **5% off** the one-time price.
- Subscription orders **ship free**, regardless of the usual free-shipping threshold.

## Pausing

A subscription can be paused for up to **3 months**. The pause must be made **at least 24 hours before the next billing cycle**, otherwise that cycle is still charged.

## Cancelling

Cancellation must be completed **at least 24 hours before the next billing cycle**. A cycle already scheduled for dispatch still ships as planned; contact support for a prorated refund if needed.

## Reactivating

Reactivating a cancelled subscription uses the **current** price and stock availability; the original subscription price is not retained.
