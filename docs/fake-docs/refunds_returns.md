# Refunds & Returns

This document covers the refund window, conditions, process, and timelines.

## Refund Window

Refund requests must be submitted within **14 days of receiving the item** (calendar days, including holidays). Requests after the deadline cannot be accepted.

## Conditions

- The item is unused and in its original, complete packaging.
- The original proof of purchase (e-invoice or order number) is attached.
- Items that are **defective or damaged in transit** are exempt from the conditions above (see "Damaged Items").

## Process

1. Log in and go to My Orders.
2. Select the order, click "Request refund", and state the reason.
3. Support reviews the request within **3 business days**.
4. Once approved, the refund reaches your original payment method within **5–7 business days**.

## Return Shipping

- Non-defective returns: the customer pays **NT$80**, deducted from the refund.
- Defective or transit-damaged returns: ACME Shop covers the shipping.

ACME Shop charges **no restocking fee on any product category**.

## Non-Refundable Items

- Downloaded digital content (e-books, software keys).
- Customized products (engraving, printing).
- Opened food or hygiene products.
