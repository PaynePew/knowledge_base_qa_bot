# Order Management

## Cancel an Order

To cancel an order with Acme Shop, please act swiftly, as orders can only be cancelled before they have been processed for shipping. Typically, this means you have a window of about one to two hours after placing your order to initiate a cancellation. To cancel, log in to your account, navigate to the "My Orders" section, and select the order you wish to cancel. If the "Cancel Order" option is available, you may proceed by clicking it, and your order will be cancelled immediately. You will receive a confirmation email shortly thereafter. If this option is not available, it means your order has already been processed and is ineligible for cancellation.

If your order has already been shipped, you will not be able to cancel it, but you can initiate a return once it arrives. Please refer to our Return Policy page for detailed instructions on how to proceed in such cases. Note that Acme Shop does not charge any fees for cancelling an order within the eligible timeframe. However, if your order includes perishable items or personalized products, these may not be eligible for cancellation or returns due to their nature. For further assistance, feel free to contact our customer support team, who are available to help with any special cases or additional inquiries regarding your order management needs.

## Change Shipping Address

To update your shipping address before your order is dispatched from Acme Shop, please follow the steps outlined below. First, log in to your Acme Shop account and navigate to the "Order Management" section, which can be found under your account settings. Here, you will see a list of your recent orders. Select the order for which you wish to change the shipping address. If your order is still in the "Processing" status, you will have the option to edit the shipping details. Click on "Edit Shipping Address" and enter the new address accurately. Make sure to save the changes once you have updated the information.

Please note that changes can only be made before the order transitions to the "Shipped" status. Typically, orders are processed within 24 to 48 hours, so it is crucial to make any address modifications as soon as possible after placing your order. If the order has already been shipped, unfortunately, we cannot alter the shipping address. In such cases, you may need to contact the carrier directly with your tracking information to explore redirection possibilities, though success is not guaranteed.

There are no fees for changing a shipping address prior to shipment. However, it is important to double-check the new address for accuracy to avoid any potential delivery issues. For any assistance or if you encounter issues while updating your address, you can reach out to our customer service team via chat or email, and they will be happy to help you.

## Combine Orders

At Acme Shop, we strive to offer flexible and convenient order management options for our valued customers. If you have placed two separate orders and would like to combine them into a single shipment, we are here to help streamline this process. To be eligible for order combination, the orders must be placed within a 12-hour window and must not have been processed for shipping yet. This ensures that both orders can be grouped together without delaying the shipping timeline.

To combine your orders, log into your Acme Shop account and navigate to the "Order History" section. Here, you will find an option to request a combination of eligible orders. Once the request is submitted, our team will review and process it within 1-2 business days. Please note that while we do not charge any additional fees for order combinations, your combined order will be subject to the standard shipping rates applicable to the total weight and dimensions of the package.

In certain edge cases, such as when one of the orders contains perishable items or items on backorder, combining may not be possible due to the specific handling requirements. If your orders include such items, our customer service team will reach out to discuss alternative solutions. We are committed to providing a seamless shopping experience, so please do not hesitate to contact our support team if you require further assistance or have any additional questions.

## Backordered Items

When an item in your order is backordered at Acme Shop, it means that the product you selected is temporarily out of stock but will be available soon. Once your order is placed, we will immediately notify you via email if any items are backordered, providing an estimated restock date. While we strive to minimize backorder situations, they can occur due to high demand or supply chain challenges. We assure you that we are working diligently with our suppliers to replenish stock as quickly as possible.

During the backorder period, you won't be charged additional fees. You have the option to either wait for the item to be restocked or cancel the backordered item at any time for a full refund. If you choose to wait, we will ship the backordered item separately at no extra shipping cost once it becomes available. For orders containing multiple items, in-stock items will be shipped immediately to avoid delays while the backordered item is processed separately. Please note that the estimated restock date is subject to change, and we will keep you updated with any adjustments.

If your backordered item has not been shipped within 30 days of the original estimated restock date, you may qualify for additional compensation or discounts on future purchases as a gesture of goodwill. If you have further questions, or need assistance with your backordered item, please contact our customer service team. We are committed to ensuring a smooth and satisfactory shopping experience.
