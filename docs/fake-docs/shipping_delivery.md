# Shipping & Delivery

This document covers domestic shipping methods, fees, and delivery times. For international orders, see "International Shipping".

## Standard Shipping

Orders to the Taiwan main island arrive **3–5 business days** after dispatch; outlying islands (Penghu, Kinmen, Matsu) take an additional **1–3 business days**.

Standard shipping costs **NT$80**; orders of **NT$1,000 or more** ship **free**.

## Express Delivery

Express delivery to main-island addresses arrives **1–2 business days** after dispatch and costs **NT$150**. Orders must be placed and paid **before 14:00** the same day. Oversized or fragile items are not eligible; the checkout page automatically shows the available options.

## Dispatch Time

Orders are dispatched within **24–48 hours** after payment confirmation; during major sale events dispatch may be delayed by 1–2 days.

## Tracking

After dispatch you receive an email with the tracking number. Track progress under **My Orders → Track Shipment**; tracking data updates within **24 hours** of dispatch.
