# Store Pickup

This document covers in-store pickup locations, timing, and requirements.

## Locations

All three stores — Taipei Xinyi, Taichung Xitun, and Kaohsiung Dream Mall — offer in-store pickup, **free of charge**.

## Timing

Orders are usually ready within **2 hours** of payment (during store opening hours). Head to the store only after the order status shows "**Ready for pickup**"; stores cannot pull stock early.

## Hold Period

In-store pickup orders (not convenience-store parcels, which follow their own 7-day rule) are held for **3 days (calendar days)** from the ready-for-pickup notification. Unclaimed orders are cancelled automatically with a **full refund**, returned to the original payment method within the standard refund timeline of **5-7 business days**.

## What to Bring

Present your **order barcode** and a **photo ID** at pickup. Someone else may collect on your behalf with the order barcode and their own photo ID.
