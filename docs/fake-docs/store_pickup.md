# Store Pickup

## Buy Online Pick Up In Store

To place an order for in-store pickup at Acme Shop, begin by browsing our online catalog and adding your desired items to the cart. During checkout, select the "Buy Online Pick Up In Store" option. You'll be prompted to choose your preferred store location based on your ZIP code to ensure availability. Once you complete your purchase, you will receive an email confirmation with your order details and estimated pickup time. Typically, orders are ready for collection within 2 hours for items in stock, but it may take up to 24 hours during peak times or for larger orders. You will receive a notification when your order is ready.

Please note that some items may not be eligible for in-store pickup due to size, availability, or shipping restrictions. These items will be marked as such on the product page. If you wish to change your pickup location after placing an order, please contact our customer service team as quickly as possible to accommodate your request. There is no additional fee for selecting in-store pickup, making it a convenient and cost-effective option.

When picking up your order, bring a copy of your confirmation email and a valid photo ID. If someone else is picking up on your behalf, they must present a copy of the confirmation email and their own photo ID. Orders should be collected within 7 days to avoid cancellation and restocking. If you encounter any issues or have further questions, our store associates and customer service team are happy to assist.

## Pickup Identification

To ensure a smooth and secure pickup experience at Acme Shop, customers are required to present valid identification when collecting their orders. When you arrive at the store, please have a government-issued photo ID ready. Acceptable forms of identification include a driver's license, passport, or state ID card. This measure helps us verify the identity of the person collecting the order and ensures that your purchase is safely handed over to the correct individual.

In cases where someone else will be picking up the order on your behalf, you must designate them as an authorized pickup person during the checkout process or by updating your order details online. The designated individual will need to present their own government-issued photo ID and a copy of the order confirmation email. Kindly note that without these documents, they will not be able to collect the order. This protocol is crucial for maintaining the security of your purchase.

Orders are typically ready for pickup within 24 hours after the order is placed. You will receive an email notification confirming that your order is ready for collection. Please ensure you pick up your order within seven days of receiving this notification. If you encounter any difficulties or need to extend the pickup period, contact our customer service team for assistance. There are no additional fees for store pickup, making it a convenient and cost-effective option for receiving your items.
