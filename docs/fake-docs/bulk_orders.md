# Bulk Orders

## Eligibility and Minimum Order

Business and institutional customers can place bulk orders for quantities of twenty-five or more units of any single product in the catalogue. Bulk orders are processed through the business account portal rather than the standard checkout flow. Customers must have a verified business account, which requires a company name, a tax identification number, and a billing contact separate from the shipping contact.

## Volume Discounts

Volume discounts apply automatically at checkout based on the line-item quantity: ten percent off at twenty-five to forty-nine units, fifteen percent off at fifty to ninety-nine units, and twenty percent off at one hundred units or more. Discounts are calculated per product line and do not stack with promotional codes or price-match adjustments. Custom pricing for orders exceeding five hundred units of a single product is available on request through the dedicated business support line.

## Fulfilment and Lead Times

Bulk orders of in-stock items ship within three business days on a pallet via freight carrier; orders containing backordered items ship as soon as every line is available unless the customer requests a split shipment. Freight delivery is curbside by default; liftgate service and inside delivery can be added for a flat surcharge quoted at checkout. The business account portal shows real-time inventory levels so customers can plan reorders before stock falls below safety thresholds.
