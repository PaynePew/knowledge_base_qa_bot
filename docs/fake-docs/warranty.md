# Warranty

## Coverage Period

At Acme Shop, we are committed to ensuring the quality and durability of our products. Our standard warranty provides coverage for a period of 12 months from the date of purchase. During this time, we cover any defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and conditions. If you encounter an issue that falls under these categories, you may be eligible for a repair, replacement, or refund at no additional cost. To initiate a warranty claim, please contact our customer service team with your purchase receipt and a detailed description of the issue.

The warranty applies only to items purchased directly from Acme Shop or authorized retailers. It does not cover damages resulting from misuse, unauthorized alterations, accidental damage, or normal wear and tear. Additionally, consumable parts such as batteries are not covered unless a defect in materials or workmanship has occurred. To maintain the validity of your warranty coverage, it is crucial to adhere to the care instructions provided with your product.

In the event of a warranty claim, our team will guide you through the necessary steps, which may include shipping the product to our service center for evaluation. If a product is deemed ineligible for warranty service, we will provide a detailed explanation, and you may choose to proceed with a paid repair option. We strive to resolve claims efficiently, typically within 14 business days from the receipt of the item. Your satisfaction is our priority, and we are here to support you throughout the warranty process.

## Warranty Claim

To file a warranty claim with Acme Shop, please follow these detailed steps to ensure a smooth process. First, verify that your product is still under warranty by checking the purchase date on your receipt or the order confirmation email. Acme Shop offers a one-year limited warranty on most products, covering manufacturing defects and malfunctions under normal use. If your product qualifies, gather the necessary documentation, including your proof of purchase, product serial number, and a detailed description of the issue. Photos or videos showcasing the defect can also be helpful in expediting the process.

Next, visit the Acme Shop warranty claim page on our website and fill out the online claim form. Please ensure all fields are completed accurately to avoid delays. Once submitted, our customer service team will review your claim within 3-5 business days and contact you via email with further instructions. If your claim is approved, you will receive a prepaid shipping label to return the defective item. Please note that Acme Shop does not cover return shipping costs for items outside of the warranty period or those with issues resulting from misuse.

If your product is deemed ineligible for a warranty claim, you will be responsible for any repair or replacement costs. We recommend reviewing our warranty policy for detailed terms and conditions. For additional questions or assistance, please contact our support team via phone or live chat. Our goal is to resolve your claim promptly and ensure your satisfaction with our products and services.
