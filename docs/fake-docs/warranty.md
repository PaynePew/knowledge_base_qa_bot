# Warranty

## Coverage Period

Acme Shop electronics include a standard manufacturer warranty of one year from the purchase date. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. Accidental damage, liquid exposure, and unauthorized repairs are explicitly excluded from coverage.

## Warranty Claim

To file a warranty claim, the customer submits the order number and a description of the fault through the support portal. An advisor issues a prepaid return label, and the defective unit is repaired or replaced free of charge once the fault is confirmed. Replacement units carry the remainder of the original warranty period rather than a fresh one-year term.
