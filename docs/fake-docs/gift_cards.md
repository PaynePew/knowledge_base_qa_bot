# Gift Cards

This document covers digital gift card denominations, validity, and usage rules.

## Denominations & Delivery

Gift cards are digital, available in **NT$500, NT$1,000, and NT$3,000**, and are delivered by **email** to the recipient after payment.

## Validity

In line with Taiwan's gift voucher regulations, gift cards have **no expiry date**.

## Gift Cards and Reward Points

- Orders **buying a gift card** do not earn reward points.
- Orders **paid with a gift card** earn points normally.

## Gift Card Restrictions

- Gift cards are non-refundable, cannot be exchanged for cash, and give no cash change.
- Unused balance stays in your account for future purchases.
- Promo codes cannot be applied to gift card purchases (see "Promo Codes").
