# Membership & Reward Points

This document covers earning, redeeming, and expiry of reward points, plus membership tiers.

## Earning Points

Earn **1 point per NT$100 spent**. Points are calculated on the **amount actually paid**: shipping fees and amounts covered by point redemption do not earn points. Points land in your account within **24 hours** of order completion (after the inspection period).

## Redeeming Points

At checkout, **1 point = NT$1** off. A single order can redeem points for at most **50% of the order total**.

## Expiry

Points are valid for **12 months** from the credit date and expire automatically. Check balances and expiry dates under **My Points**.

## Referrals

When a friend registers through your referral link and **completes a first purchase**, you receive **50 points**.

## Restrictions

Points cannot be exchanged for cash or transferred between accounts. If an order is refunded, points redeemed on it and points earned from it are both reclaimed.

## Membership Tiers

| Tier | Qualification | Benefits |
|---|---|---|
| Member | Free on registration | Standard point earning |
| Gold | **NT$30,000** spent in the last 12 months | Free shipping with no minimum; double points during your birthday month |
| Platinum | **NT$100,000** spent in the last 12 months | All Gold benefits; free upgrade to express delivery; dedicated support line |
