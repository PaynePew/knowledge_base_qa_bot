# Contact Support

This document lists ACME Shop's support channels, service hours, and response times - use it as a quick reference for choosing a contact method and knowing when to expect a reply.

## Channels

- **Live chat**: the chat widget at the bottom-right of the website.
- **Email**: support@acme-shop.example
- **Phone**: 02-1234-5678

All three channels reach the support team directly; pick whichever you prefer.

## Service Hours

- Phone and email support: Monday–Friday, **09:00–18:00**.
- Live chat: weekday hours above, plus Saturday **10:00–17:00**.

Support is unavailable on national holidays.

## Response Time

Emails are answered within **1 business day**. Live chat responds in real time during service hours; messages sent outside service hours are queued as offline messages and handled in order during the next service window.
