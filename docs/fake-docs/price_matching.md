# Price Matching

## Price Match Eligibility

Acme Shop matches a lower advertised price on an identical in-stock item from a short list of approved major retailers. The competing item must be sold and shipped by the retailer directly rather than a third-party marketplace seller, and the lower price must be available to the general public rather than a members-only or clearance price.

## Requesting a Price Match

A price match is requested within fourteen days of purchase by submitting a link to the competitor's listing, and an approved match is refunded as the difference to the original payment method. Each customer is limited to three approved price matches in any thirty-day period to discourage reselling.
