# Price Matching

## Price Match Eligibility

At Acme Shop, we strive to offer competitive pricing on all our products. Our Price Match Guarantee ensures that you get the best deal available. To qualify for a price match, the product must be identical to the one offered by a competitor—this includes brand, model number, and color. The item must be in stock and available for immediate purchase both at Acme Shop and the competing retailer. Price matching is applicable only to competitors' prices advertised online or in physical stores within the same country where you are making your purchase from Acme Shop.

To initiate a price match request, please contact our customer service team either through our website or by phone within 14 days of your purchase. You will need to provide proof of the competitor's lower price, such as a digital or physical advertisement. Our team will verify the competitor's price and confirm that it meets all eligibility conditions. Please note that price matching does not apply to limited-time promotions such as flash sales, clearance sales, or items that are part of a bundle offer. 

Furthermore, price matching excludes membership-based pricing, auction sites, and unauthorized retailers. Once your request is approved, any price difference will be refunded via your original payment method within 5-7 business days. Acme Shop reserves the right to decline a price match at our discretion, but we are committed to ensuring fair and reasonable evaluations of all requests.

## Requesting a Price Match

At Acme Shop, we are committed to offering competitive prices to our valued customers. If you find a lower price on an identical item from a qualifying retailer, you can submit a price-match request to enjoy the same savings with us. To initiate a price-match request, please contact our customer service team via email or phone within 14 days of your purchase. Be prepared to provide proof of the lower price, such as a screenshot or a direct link to the competitor’s offer, ensuring the product matches in brand, model number, and specifications.

Eligibility for price matching requires that the competitor must be a legitimate, authorized retailer with the item in stock and available for immediate purchase. Please note that we do not price match against auction-based sites or discount membership clubs. Additionally, special promotions, clearance, bundled offers, or limited-time sales are not eligible for price matching. Once your request is received, our team will verify the details and, if approved, process a refund of the price difference to your original method of payment. This process typically takes 3–5 business days.

In certain cases, such as discrepancies in shipping fees or taxes, additional verification may be required, potentially extending the processing time. Rest assured, no additional fees will be applied for processing a price-match request. Our goal is to ensure your shopping experience with Acme Shop remains both affordable and satisfying.
