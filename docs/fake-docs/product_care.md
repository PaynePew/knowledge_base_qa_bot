# Product Care

## Cleaning Instructions

Most Acme Shop kitchen and dining products can be cleaned with warm water and mild dish soap using a soft cloth or sponge. Abrasive scrubbers and bleach-based cleaners are not recommended as they can scratch coatings and discolour finishes over time. Products marked dishwasher-safe on their packaging and product page can be placed on the top rack only; high-heat drying cycles may warp plastic components, so air drying is preferred for items used daily.

## Storage Guidelines

Store kitchen tools in a dry environment away from direct heat sources such as stovetops and ovens; prolonged exposure to heat above sixty degrees Celsius can degrade adhesives and silicone seals. Knives and cutting boards should be stored separately from products with metallic surfaces to prevent scratching. Items with non-stick coatings should be stored with felt or paper liners between them if stacked, so the surfaces do not contact each other.

## Warranty Implications

Using a product outside the recommended care instructions does not automatically void the warranty, but damage attributable to misuse is assessed separately from manufacturing defects during a warranty claim. The assessment team reviews photographs submitted with a claim and makes a determination within three business days. Products that have been modified, repaired by a third party, or used in a commercial kitchen setting where the listing specifies household use only are ineligible for warranty replacement under the standard coverage terms.
