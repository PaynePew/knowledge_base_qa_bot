# Customer Support

## Contact Channels

Acme Shop offers support through three channels: live chat available on the website from eight in the morning to ten at night Eastern time every day of the year, email with a guaranteed first response within four business hours, and phone support from nine to six Eastern time on weekdays only. The live-chat channel shows estimated wait times before connecting; wait times typically run under two minutes during off-peak hours and up to eight minutes between noon and two in the afternoon on weekdays.

## Escalation Process

If the first-tier agent cannot resolve an issue within one interaction, it is escalated to the second-tier specialist team with a reference number the customer can use to follow up. Escalated cases are prioritised by recency and order value, and the specialist contacts the customer within one business day. Customers can also request escalation directly from the support portal after any closed ticket by using the "reopen and escalate" option within seven days of closure.

## Self-Service Resources

The help centre contains step-by-step guides for the most common account, order, and returns tasks. Guides are updated within forty-eight hours of any policy change. A virtual assistant in the help centre can answer routine questions about order status, tracking, and return eligibility without opening a live ticket; it hands off to a human agent automatically when the query falls outside its scope.
