# International Shipping

## Supported Countries

Acme Shop ships to Canada, the United Kingdom, the European Union, Australia, and Japan. Shipping to other destinations is not yet available, though the list of supported countries expands periodically. Orders to supported countries display the landed cost, including duties, before checkout so there are no surprises at delivery.

## Customs and Duties

For supported destinations, import duties and taxes are collected at checkout under a delivered-duty-paid arrangement, so the carrier never bills the recipient on arrival. The exception is oversized freight, where duties are assessed by the destination customs office and billed separately because their value is hard to estimate in advance.

## International Delivery Times

International orders ship within two business days and then take seven to fourteen business days in transit depending on the destination and local customs processing. Remote regions outside major metropolitan areas may add a further three to five business days.
