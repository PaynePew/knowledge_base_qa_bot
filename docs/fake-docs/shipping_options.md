# Shipping Options

## Standard Delivery

At Acme Shop, we strive to ensure your shopping experience is as seamless as possible, and our Standard Delivery option is designed to offer you a reliable and cost-effective shipping method. Typically, orders processed with Standard Delivery will arrive at your doorstep within 3 to 7 business days from the date of dispatch. Please note that dispatch occurs within 24 to 48 hours after your order has been confirmed, unless otherwise stated during peak shopping periods, such as holidays or sales events, which may extend processing times by an additional 1 to 2 days.

The cost of Standard Delivery is calculated based on the total weight of your order and the destination address. For most domestic orders, Standard Delivery fees start at $5.99, but orders over $50 qualify for free Standard Delivery, providing a great incentive to maximize your shopping experience with us. For international orders, additional shipping charges may apply, and delivery times can be extended to 10 to 14 business days, depending on the destination country and any customs processes involved.

In terms of eligibility, most products are available for Standard Delivery unless specified otherwise on the product page. Certain oversized or heavy items might incur additional charges or may not be eligible for standard shipping. We recommend reviewing the shipping details specific to each item. Should you encounter any issues or have further questions, our customer support team is available to assist you with comprehensive solutions tailored to your needs.

## Expedited Delivery

At Acme Shop, we understand the importance of getting your orders quickly, which is why we offer expedited and next-day delivery options to meet your urgent needs. Our expedited delivery service typically ensures your order arrives within 2-3 business days, perfect for when you need your items in a hurry but not instantly. For those in immediate need, our next-day delivery option guarantees your package arrives the very next business day. To qualify for these services, orders must be placed before 3 PM local time from Monday to Friday, as this allows us sufficient time to process and dispatch your items.

Please note that expedited and next-day delivery options are available only for select items and destinations. During checkout, eligible products will display these options automatically. Additional fees apply for both services, calculated based on the weight, size, and destination of your package. For expedited delivery, fees start at $8.99, while next-day delivery begins at $19.99. Certain remote or rural areas may experience longer transit times and a potential surcharge, which will be indicated at checkout.

In rare circumstances such as extreme weather conditions or other unforeseen disruptions, delivery times might be affected. In such cases, our customer service team will proactively inform you of any delays and work to ensure your package arrives as soon as possible. If you have any questions or require assistance with your order, our support team is available 24/7 to help.

## Order Tracking

At Acme Shop, we understand the importance of knowing where your order is at all times. Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email containing your unique tracking number and a link to the carrier’s website. Simply click on the link or enter your tracking number on the carrier's website to view the current status of your shipment. You can also track your order by logging into your Acme Shop account. Navigate to the "My Orders" section, select the order you wish to track, and click on the "Track Shipment" button to get real-time updates.

Typically, tracking information becomes available within 24-48 hours after your order has been shipped. Please note that tracking updates may occasionally be delayed due to processing times at the carrier’s end. If you notice any discrepancies or if your order hasn’t arrived within the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer support team for assistance. We are here to help ensure your shopping experience is smooth and satisfactory.

For international orders, tracking might be limited depending on the carrier and the destination country's postal service. In some cases, additional customs processing times might delay your shipment. If your order is eligible for expedited shipping, you can choose this option at checkout for an additional fee, which includes faster processing and more frequent tracking updates. If you have any questions or require further assistance, please reach out to our support team, who are ready to assist you with any shipment tracking concerns.
