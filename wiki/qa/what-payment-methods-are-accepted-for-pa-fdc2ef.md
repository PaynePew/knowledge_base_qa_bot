We accept the following payment methods: VISA, MasterCard, and JCB credit cards, paid as a single charge or in installments for qualifying orders [Source: credit-cards#credit-cards]; convenience store payment codes, with a NT$20,000 per-order limit and payment due within 3 days of placing the order [Source: convenience-store-payment-codes#convenience-store-payment-codes]; mobile payments via Apple Pay and Google Pay [Source: mobile-payments#mobile-payments]; and cash on delivery, available when combined with convenience store pickup on the main island only [Source: cash-on-delivery#cash-on-delivery]. Bank transfers, checks, and foreign-currency payments are not accepted [Source: not-accepted#not-accepted].
