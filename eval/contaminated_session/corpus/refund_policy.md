# Refund Policy

## Refund Window
You may request a refund within 14 days of the delivery date. Approved
refunds are credited to your original payment method within 5 business days
of approval.

## Store Credit Refunds
If you return an item without a receipt, we issue store credit instead of a
cash refund. Store credit is emailed to you within 2 business days and does
not expire.
