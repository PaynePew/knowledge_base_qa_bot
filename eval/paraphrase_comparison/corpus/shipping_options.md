# Shipping Options

## Standard Delivery

Standard delivery is free on orders over fifty dollars and takes three to five business days within the continental United States. Orders below the free-shipping threshold pay a flat four-dollar fee. Standard delivery does not include weekend handling, so an order placed on Friday afternoon begins processing the following Monday.

## Expedited Delivery

Expedited delivery guarantees arrival within two business days for a twelve-dollar surcharge. Orders must be placed before two o'clock in the afternoon local time to ship the same day; later orders ship the next morning. Expedited delivery is unavailable for oversized freight items and for destinations outside the contiguous states.

## Order Tracking

Every shipment generates a tracking number that is emailed to the customer once the carrier scans the parcel. Customers can also view live tracking status from the order history page in their account. Tracking information may take up to twelve hours to populate after the shipping confirmation email is sent.
