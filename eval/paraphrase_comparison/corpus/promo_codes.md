# Promo Codes

## Applying a Code

Applying a promotional code at checkout on Acme Shop is a straightforward process designed to enhance your shopping experience. To begin, once you have added your desired items to your cart, proceed to the checkout page. Here, you will find a designated field labeled "Promo Code" or "Discount Code." Enter your promotional code precisely as provided, paying close attention to any case sensitivity or special characters. After entering the code, click the "Apply" button to ensure it is validated. If the code is valid and applicable, you will see the discount reflected in your order summary, reducing your total purchase amount before you proceed with payment.

It's important to note that promotional codes may come with specific eligibility conditions. These conditions can include minimum purchase requirements, product exclusions, or expiration dates. Always check the terms and conditions associated with your code to ensure it is applicable to your order. If you encounter an error message, double-check the code for accuracy and ensure it hasn't expired or been applied to an ineligible item.

In rare cases, you might face issues with a promo code if there's a technical glitch or if the code has already been used. If the problem persists, contact our customer support team for assistance. They will be happy to help resolve any issues or provide guidance on alternative discounts. Remember, promo codes cannot be combined with other offers unless explicitly stated, and they do not apply to shipping fees or taxes.

## Stacking Rules

At Acme Shop, we strive to offer our customers the best deals possible while maintaining a fair and consistent promotional policy. Our promotional codes are designed to provide flexibility, but it's important to understand how they can be combined—also known as "stacking"—to maximize your savings. Typically, our system allows only one promotional code per order. This ensures that the discounts are applied in a manner that is both efficient and beneficial for all parties involved. However, there are special instances where stacking can occur.

Certain promotional offers, such as those tied to specific product categories or minimum purchase thresholds, may be eligible for stacking with a free shipping code. For example, if you have a 10% off code for electronics and a separate free shipping promo, you can apply both to your order if it meets the eligibility requirements. It's important to note that while free shipping codes can often be stacked, percentage-off or dollar-off codes cannot be combined with other similar discount codes.

To ensure a smooth experience, all eligible codes must be entered at checkout before completing your purchase. The system will automatically apply the best possible discount combination available. If you encounter any issues or uncertainties about stacking rules, our customer service team is available to assist. Please reach out within 14 days of your purchase for any promo code-related inquiries. Remember, promotional codes cannot be applied retroactively, so make sure all details are correct before finalizing your order.
