# Account Security

## Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is an essential feature offered by Acme Shop to enhance the security of your account. Setting up 2FA adds an extra layer of protection by requiring a second form of verification when you log in, beyond just your password. To enable 2FA, first ensure you have a compatible mobile device and a valid phone number linked to your account. Navigate to the "Account Security" section in your account settings and select "Enable Two-Factor Authentication." You will be prompted to choose a verification method: either via SMS or an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the setup, which typically takes only a few minutes.

Once 2FA is active, every time you log in, you'll be prompted to enter a code sent to your chosen verification method. This process is quick, usually taking less than a minute, but significantly boosts your account's security. There are no fees for using 2FA on Acme Shop. However, ensure your mobile device is accessible, as losing access may require additional steps for account recovery. In such cases, our customer support can assist you, but it may take up to 24 hours to verify your identity and restore access. For users traveling abroad, ensure that your chosen verification method works internationally to prevent login issues. By enabling 2FA, you significantly reduce the risk of unauthorized access to your account, safeguarding your personal information and purchases.

## Suspicious Activity

When Acme Shop detects suspicious login activity on your account, our primary concern is your security. We have robust systems in place that monitor for irregularities such as logins from unfamiliar locations, devices, or IP addresses. If suspicious activity is identified, we will immediately send an alert to your registered email address and, if applicable, via SMS to your mobile device. This alert will contain details of the login attempt, including the device type and location, along with a prompt to verify whether the activity was legitimate.

If the login attempt is unrecognized, we recommend that you promptly change your password to prevent unauthorized access. You can do this by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and following the instructions. In some cases, to protect your account, Acme Shop may temporarily lock it until verification is completed. During this period, you will be unable to make purchases or access certain features until we confirm your identity. This process is typically resolved within 24 hours after you contact our support team and provide the necessary information.

There are no fees involved for resolving suspicious activity alerts. However, it's crucial that you act quickly to prevent potential misuse. For added security, we encourage enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on your account, which provides an extra layer of protection. If you encounter any difficulties or have further questions about suspicious activity, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you.

## Managing Trusted Devices

At Acme Shop, we prioritize your account security, and managing your trusted devices is a crucial part of safeguarding your personal information. To view and manage the devices you trust, start by logging into your Acme Shop account and navigating to the "Account Settings" section. From there, select "Security Settings" and then click on "Trusted Devices." This page will display a list of devices currently marked as trusted, including details like the device type, the last login date, and the location of access. Review this list regularly to ensure that only devices you recognize are listed as trusted.

If you need to remove a device, simply click the "Remove" button next to the device you no longer use or recognize. Once removed, the next time someone attempts to log in from that device, they will be required to complete additional security steps, such as entering a verification code sent to your registered email or phone number. This process is immediate and incurs no fee. However, keep in mind that removing a device may interrupt your access if you're using it for auto-login features, so be sure to have your login credentials handy.

In cases where you suspect unauthorized access, it is advisable to change your password immediately and remove all trusted devices to reset your security settings. If you need further assistance or encounter any issues while managing your trusted devices, our customer support team is available 24/7 to help you navigate these steps and ensure your account remains secure.
