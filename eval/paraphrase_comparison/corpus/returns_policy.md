# Returns Policy

## Return Window

At Acme Shop, we understand that sometimes a purchase may not meet your expectations, which is why we offer a flexible return window to ensure your satisfaction. Customers have 30 days from the date of delivery to return items for a refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, the item must be in its original condition, unused, and with all tags and packaging intact. We encourage you to inspect your purchase promptly upon receipt to ensure it meets your standards.

To initiate a return, please visit our Returns Center on our website and enter your order details. You'll receive a prepaid return shipping label via email, making the process seamless. Please note that while we cover the cost of return shipping for most items, oversized items or those shipped from international locations may incur a return fee, which will be deducted from your refund. Refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item.

Certain items, such as perishable goods, customized products, and intimate apparel, are not eligible for returns. Additionally, any items marked "final sale" at the time of purchase are ineligible for return. If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery for a free replacement or refund. We value your business and strive to make your shopping experience as pleasant and hassle-free as possible.

## Refund Processing Time

At Acme Shop, we strive to make your shopping experience seamless, including handling returns and refunds efficiently. Once we receive your returned item at our warehouse, the refund process typically takes 5 to 7 business days. This timeframe allows our team to thoroughly inspect the returned product to ensure it meets our return eligibility conditions, which include being in its original packaging and condition. Please note that during peak seasons, such as holidays, processing times may extend slightly due to increased volumes.

Refunds are credited to the original payment method used for the purchase. If you paid via credit card, please allow an additional 3 to 5 business days for your bank to process and post the refund to your account. For PayPal and other digital wallets, the refund should appear in your account within 24 to 48 hours after processing. In cases where a gift card was used, the amount will be credited back to the card. Please ensure the card is retained until the refund is finalized.

Occasionally, edge cases such as items returned beyond the allowed return period or those that do not meet our return criteria may require additional review, extending the refund process. In such instances, our customer support team will reach out to you with further instructions. No restocking fees are charged for eligible returns, ensuring you receive the full refund amount. For further assistance, our customer service team is available to address any concerns or questions you may have regarding your refund.

## Restocking Fee

At Acme Shop, we strive to provide a seamless return experience for our customers. However, certain items are subject to a restocking fee when returned. A restocking fee of 15% of the purchase price applies to all electronics, large appliances, and furniture items. This fee is necessary to cover the costs associated with processing and handling returns for these specific product categories. For example, if you purchased an electronic device for $500, the restocking fee deducted from your refund would be $75. This policy helps us maintain competitive pricing while efficiently managing our inventory.

To be eligible for a return without additional complications, items must be returned in their original packaging and condition within 30 days of the delivery date. Please ensure all accessories, manuals, and parts are included to avoid any further deductions. Once we receive your returned item, our team will inspect it to confirm it meets the eligibility criteria. The restocking fee will be applied before your refund is processed, which typically takes 7-10 business days.

There are certain exceptions where the restocking fee may be waived. If the return is due to an error on our part—such as receiving a defective product or an incorrect item—the restocking fee will not be charged. In such cases, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible to initiate the return process and ensure a smooth resolution.
