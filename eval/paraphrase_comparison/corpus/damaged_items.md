# Damaged Items

## Reporting Damage

An item that arrives damaged should be reported within seventy-two hours of delivery through the support portal, with photographs of both the product and the outer packaging. Reporting within the window qualifies the order for a free replacement or full refund without the usual return shipping step.

## Replacement Process

Once a damage report is approved, a replacement ships by expedited delivery at no charge, and the customer keeps or disposes of the damaged unit rather than returning it. If the same product is out of stock, the customer may choose a refund or wait for the next restock without losing their place.
