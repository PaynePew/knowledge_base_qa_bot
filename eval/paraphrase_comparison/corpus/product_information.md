# Product Information

## Stock Availability

Each product page shows a live stock count drawn from the nearest fulfilment centre. When fewer than five units remain the page displays a low-stock banner, and when the count reaches zero customers can join a restock waitlist that emails them the moment inventory returns. Stock figures refresh every fifteen minutes, so a number seen at checkout may differ slightly from the page viewed minutes earlier.

## Product Reviews

Verified-purchase reviews carry a badge and are weighted more heavily in the average rating than unverified reviews. A review can be submitted up to ninety days after delivery, and reviews containing profanity or personal information are removed by moderation within one business day.

## Size Guide

Apparel listings include a size guide measured in both inches and centimetres, taken flat across the garment rather than on the body. When a customer's measurements fall between two listed sizes, the guide recommends sizing up for outerwear and sizing down for fitted base layers.
