# Product Information

## Stock Availability

To check whether a product is in stock at Acme Shop, simply navigate to the product page of the item you are interested in. The current stock status will be clearly displayed near the product title, typically in a format such as "In Stock," "Low Stock," or "Out of Stock." If the item is available, you can proceed to add it to your cart and complete the purchase. If the product is labeled as "Low Stock," we recommend acting quickly as it may sell out soon. For items marked "Out of Stock," we offer a convenient restock alert feature to help keep you informed.

Setting up a restock alert is easy and free of charge. On the product page for an out-of-stock item, click the "Notify Me" button, and enter your email address or phone number when prompted. Once the item becomes available again, we will send you an alert immediately. Please note that opting for this service does not reserve the item for you; availability is on a first-come, first-served basis. While we aim to restock popular items frequently, exact timeframes can vary depending on supply chain factors. If an item is discontinued or unlikely to be restocked, this will be indicated on the product page, and restock alerts will not be available.

## Product Reviews

At Acme Shop, we value our customers' opinions and strive to provide transparent and trustworthy product reviews to assist you in making informed purchasing decisions. Our review collection process begins after your purchase is confirmed and the product has been delivered. You will receive an email invitation to leave a review typically within 7 to 14 days of delivery. This timeframe allows you to experience the product firsthand before sharing your thoughts. To ensure authenticity, only verified buyers can submit reviews, which helps maintain the integrity of our review system.

Once you submit a review, it undergoes a moderation process to ensure compliance with our content guidelines. This process typically takes 24 to 48 hours. We check for inappropriate language, personal information, and relevance to the product. Rest assured, we do not alter the content of your review. Approved reviews are then displayed on the product page, contributing to the overall star rating and providing valuable insights for future customers.

Our review system is free of charge and open to all customers who have completed a purchase. In rare cases where a review is not published, it may be due to violations of our guidelines, such as promotional content or conflicts of interest. If you have further questions or encounter issues with your review, our customer support team is available to assist you.

## Size Guide

At Acme Shop, we understand the importance of finding the perfect fit for your purchases, which is why we've developed comprehensive sizing charts to guide you in making informed decisions. To use our sizing charts effectively, begin by locating the product category on the specific product page; each category, such as apparel, footwear, or accessories, has its tailored chart to ensure accuracy. For clothing, you'll find measurements for chest, waist, hips, and inseam, often in both inches and centimeters. Our footwear charts typically include foot length and width measurements, along with conversions for international sizes.

To determine your size, use a flexible tape measure and follow our detailed instructions on taking measurements accurately at home. For instance, when measuring your chest, ensure the tape is snug but not too tight and parallel to the floor. Compare your measurements with the chart to determine the best size for you. If you're between sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for comfort. Remember, different brands might have slight variations, so always refer to our specific product page for the most precise information. If you have further questions or need personalized assistance, our customer service team is ready to help via chat or email. Additionally, we offer free exchanges within 30 days should you find your selected size is not quite right, ensuring your shopping experience remains seamless and satisfactory.
