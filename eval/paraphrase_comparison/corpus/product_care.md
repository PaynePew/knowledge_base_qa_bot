# Product Care

## Cleaning Instructions

To ensure the longevity and aesthetic appeal of your Acme Shop products, proper cleaning and care are essential. For fabric items, such as clothing or upholstery, we recommend using a gentle detergent and cold water. Hand washing is ideal, but if machine washing is necessary, use a delicate cycle and place items in a protective mesh bag. Always air dry fabric products to prevent shrinkage or damage from high heat. For stains, pre-treat affected areas with a mild stain remover before washing, and avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that could degrade the material.

For electronic items purchased from Acme Shop, it's crucial to unplug them before cleaning. Use a soft, dry microfiber cloth to wipe down surfaces, ensuring that no moisture enters the device. If deeper cleaning is necessary, lightly dampen the cloth with distilled water or a specific electronic-safe cleaning solution, avoiding direct application to the product. Pay special attention to ports and crevices, using compressed air to remove any dust or debris. Always allow the device to dry completely before replugging or using it.

For wooden or metal items, a soft cloth or duster is typically sufficient. If a deeper clean is required, a slightly damp cloth with a small amount of mild soap can be used, followed by a dry cloth to remove any excess moisture. For metal items, ensure they are thoroughly dried to prevent rust. Acme Shop does not charge any fees for guidance on product care, and our customer service team is available for any specific queries or concerns.

## Storage Guidelines

To ensure the longevity of products purchased from Acme Shop, proper storage is essential. For items sensitive to environmental conditions, such as electronics, cosmetics, and textiles, it is crucial to maintain a stable environment. Electronics should be stored in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight and moisture, which can damage internal components. Ideally, maintain a temperature between 50°F and 77°F (10°C and 25°C) with relative humidity below 60%. Cosmetics, particularly those with natural ingredients, should be kept away from heat and direct sunlight to prevent degradation. Refrigeration may be necessary for certain organic or perishable beauty products; check individual packaging for specific instructions.

Textiles, including clothing and linens, are best stored in a clean, dry area to prevent mold and mildew. Use breathable fabric bags for items like wool or silk to protect them from dust while allowing airflow. Rotate seasonal items every six months to avoid long-term creasing and potential fabric degradation. For furniture and home goods, avoid placing items in damp basements or attics where temperature fluctuations occur. If storage fees apply for larger items, our customer service team can assist in finding the most economical option available.

By following these guidelines, customers can significantly extend the life and quality of their purchased items. For specific product care instructions, refer to the care label or user manual included with your purchase. If you have any queries or encounter issues, our support team is readily available to provide assistance.

## Warranty Implications

At Acme Shop, we stand by the quality of our products, offering warranties that cover manufacturing defects and certain failures. However, it is crucial to understand that improper care or misuse of products can void this warranty coverage. To maintain eligibility, customers must adhere to the care instructions provided with each product. For instance, if a clothing item specifies hand washing only, using a washing machine could result in damage that would not be covered by the warranty. Similarly, electronic devices exposed to conditions outside their operational limits—such as extreme temperatures or moisture—may not be eligible for warranty claims if they malfunction.

When filing a warranty claim, customers are required to provide proof that the product has been maintained according to our guidelines. This could involve presenting receipts of professional cleaning or servicing where applicable. Claims must be made within the warranty period, which typically ranges from one to two years, depending on the product category. Acme Shop reserves the right to deny claims where evidence of improper care is evident. In some cases, a fee may be involved in repairing items that have been improperly maintained.

Edge cases, such as accidental damage or wear and tear, are generally not covered under warranty but may be eligible for repair services at a discounted rate. To ensure full warranty benefits, we recommend reviewing the specific care instructions and warranty terms associated with your purchase. For further clarification, our customer service team is always ready to assist with any questions regarding product care and warranty implications.
