# Loyalty Program

## Earning Points

At Acme Shop, we value your loyalty and offer a rewarding experience through our Acme Rewards program. Earning points is simple and straightforward, allowing you to accumulate rewards with every purchase. For every dollar you spend on eligible purchases, you earn 1 Acme Reward point. Eligible purchases include all products except gift cards, taxes, and shipping fees. To start earning points, ensure you are logged into your Acme Shop account at the time of purchase. Points are automatically credited to your account within 24 hours of the transaction completion.

In addition to earning points through purchases, Acme Shop offers various opportunities to boost your points balance. For instance, you can earn bonus points by participating in promotional events, such as double-point days or special anniversary sales, which are announced via our newsletter and social media channels. Furthermore, you can also earn points by referring friends to Acme Shop. For every friend who signs up and makes their first purchase using your referral link, you receive 50 bonus points.

Please note that points are valid for 12 months from the date they are credited to your account, and they cannot be redeemed for cash. If you return a purchase, the corresponding points will be deducted from your account. Keep track of your points balance and expiration dates easily by logging into your account. We aim to make your shopping experience with Acme Shop not only enjoyable but also rewarding.

## Redeeming Points

At Acme Shop, redeeming your loyalty points for account credits is a straightforward process designed to enhance your shopping experience. To begin, ensure you are logged into your Acme Shop account. Navigate to the "Loyalty Program" section from your account dashboard. Here, you will see your total available points and the corresponding value in account credits. To redeem points, select the "Redeem Points" option, and choose the amount you wish to convert into credits. It’s important to note that points can be redeemed in increments of 100, with each increment equivalent to $1 in account credit.

Once you confirm the redemption, the account credits should appear in your account within 24 hours. These credits can be used immediately for future purchases and will automatically be applied at checkout. Keep in mind, to be eligible for point redemption, your account must have a minimum balance of 500 points. Additionally, there are no fees associated with redeeming points for account credits, ensuring that you receive the full benefit of your hard-earned points.

If you encounter any issues during the redemption process, such as points not reflecting as credits or encountering an error message, please contact our customer support team. They are available 24/7 to assist you and ensure a smooth transaction. Remember, account credits from redeemed points are non-transferable and cannot be exchanged for cash, so plan your redemptions accordingly.

## Membership Tiers

Acme Shop offers a rewarding Loyalty Program designed to enhance your shopping experience through three distinct membership tiers: Silver, Gold, and Platinum. Each tier is crafted to provide increasing levels of benefits, with eligibility based on your annual spending with us. Upon joining the program, all members start at the Silver tier, which is automatically granted when you spend a minimum of $500 within a calendar year. Silver members enjoy benefits such as a 5% discount on all purchases and early access to select sales events.

Moving up to the Gold tier requires an annual expenditure of $2,000. Gold members receive all the benefits of the Silver tier, along with a 10% discount on purchases, free standard shipping on all orders, and exclusive access to limited-edition products. Additionally, Gold members are granted priority customer service, ensuring quicker resolutions to inquiries and issues. To maintain Gold status, your spending must remain at this level each year.

The pinnacle of our program is the Platinum tier, accessed by spending $5,000 annually. Platinum members enjoy a 15% discount, free express shipping, and VIP access to private sales and events. This tier also includes a personal shopping assistant to help you find the perfect products and a complimentary annual gift tailored to your preferences. To retain Platinum status, a continued yearly spend of $5,000 is required. Memberships are reviewed annually, and if you do not meet the spending requirements, you will be placed in the tier that matches your most recent annual spend.
