# Loyalty Program

## Earning Points

Acme Rewards members earn one point for every dollar spent on eligible merchandise, excluding taxes, shipping, and gift-card purchases. Points post to the account two business days after an order ships rather than at checkout, so returned items never leave orphaned points behind. Members earn double points during the first seven days after a birthday recorded in their profile.

## Redeeming Points

Points are redeemed in blocks of one hundred, where one hundred points equal a five-dollar account credit applied at checkout. Redemptions cannot be combined with clearance pricing, and the credit is forfeited if the order is later returned for a refund. Unused points expire eighteen months after they were earned.

## Membership Tiers

There are three tiers: Silver from zero points, Gold after five thousand lifetime points, and Platinum after twenty thousand lifetime points. Gold members receive free expedited shipping on every order, while Platinum members add a dedicated support line and early access to seasonal sales. Tier status is reviewed annually and a member who has not earned points in the prior twelve months drops one tier.
