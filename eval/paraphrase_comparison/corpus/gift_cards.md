# Gift Cards

## Purchase and Delivery

At Acme Shop, purchasing both digital and physical gift cards is a straightforward process designed to offer flexibility and convenience. To buy a digital gift card, simply visit our Gift Cards page, select the desired card value, and enter the recipient's email address during checkout. Digital gift cards are typically delivered within an hour of purchase, but please allow up to 24 hours in case of high demand. If you do not receive the gift card within this timeframe, check your spam or promotions folders, or contact our customer service team for assistance. Digital gift cards do not incur any delivery fees.

For physical gift cards, navigate to the same Gift Cards page, choose the card value, and enter the recipient's postal address at checkout. Physical gift cards are dispatched via standard shipping and usually arrive within 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are also available for an additional fee, ensuring delivery within 2-3 business days. Please note that physical gift card purchases are currently available only within the continental United States. Always double-check the delivery address and ensure it is correct, as Acme Shop cannot reroute gift cards once they have been dispatched. Whether sending a digital or physical gift card, rest assured that both options are redeemable online and in-store, offering your recipient the ultimate flexibility in their shopping experience.

## Redeeming a Gift Card

To redeem a gift card at Acme Shop, begin by adding your desired items to the shopping cart. Once you have finished selecting products, proceed to the checkout page. Here, you will find a designated field labeled "Gift Card or Discount Code." Enter your unique gift card code into this field and click "Apply." The system will automatically deduct the gift card balance from your total purchase amount. If the gift card balance exceeds your order total, the remaining balance will be saved for future purchases. Conversely, if the order total surpasses the gift card value, you will need to pay the difference using another payment method.

Please ensure your gift card is valid and has not expired before attempting to redeem it. Our gift cards typically have a one-year validity period from the date of purchase. Additionally, be aware that certain items, such as gift cards themselves and limited edition products, may not be eligible for purchase using a gift card. No additional fees are associated with using a gift card at checkout, so you can apply the full balance without any deductions.

In cases where the gift card code is not working, double-check for any typing errors or ensure that the card has not been previously redeemed or expired. If you encounter persistent issues, please contact our customer support team for assistance. They are available to help resolve any technical difficulties or answer any questions you may have regarding gift card redemption.

## Lost Gift Cards

If you have misplaced or lost your Acme Shop gift card, we're here to help. First, it's important to understand that our ability to replace lost gift cards is contingent upon specific conditions being met. For us to process a replacement, you must provide proof of purchase, such as a receipt or confirmation email, and the original payment method used. This helps us verify the gift card's original value and transaction details. If you have registered your gift card on our website, the replacement process is significantly streamlined as we can easily verify the card details in our system.

Once you have the necessary documentation, please contact our customer support team either through our online chat or by calling our dedicated helpline. Our team will guide you through the steps to initiate a replacement request. Please allow up to 10 business days for us to process your request and issue a replacement card. Note that a nominal processing fee of $5 may apply for the reissuance of a lost gift card.

In certain edge cases, such as if the gift card was part of a promotional offer or if it has been partially used, additional verification steps may be required, and replacement is not guaranteed. We recommend keeping your gift card information secure and registering it on our website immediately after purchase to safeguard against potential loss. If you have any questions or need further assistance, our customer service team is always ready to help.
