# Gift Cards

## Purchase and Delivery

Digital gift cards are emailed to the recipient within fifteen minutes of purchase, while physical gift cards ship as a standard parcel and follow the usual delivery timelines. A gift card can be scheduled to deliver on a future date up to one year ahead, which is useful for birthdays and holidays.

## Redeeming a Gift Card

A gift card balance is applied at checkout by entering the card number and PIN, and any order total above the balance is charged to a second payment method. Gift cards never expire and incur no dormancy fees, so an unused balance stays available indefinitely.

## Lost Gift Cards

A lost physical gift card can be replaced only if the customer provides the original purchase receipt or order number, and the remaining balance is transferred to a new card. A gift card whose balance has already been spent cannot be reissued.
