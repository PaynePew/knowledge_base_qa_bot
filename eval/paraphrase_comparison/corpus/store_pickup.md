# Store Pickup

## Buy Online Pick Up In Store

Items marked available for pickup can be reserved online and collected from a chosen store, usually ready within two hours during opening hours. The order page shows a ready-for-pickup status and sends an email once the items are waiting at the counter.

## Pickup Identification

Collecting an order requires the order number and a government-issued photo identification that matches the name on the order. A nominated alternate pickup person can collect instead if their name is added to the order before the items are released.
