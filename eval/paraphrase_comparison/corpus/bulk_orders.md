# Bulk Orders

## Eligibility and Minimum Order

At Acme Shop, we strive to cater to businesses and organizations that require larger quantities of our products by offering a convenient bulk order option. To qualify for bulk orders, customers must be registered businesses, non-profit organizations, educational institutions, or government agencies. Individuals or unregistered entities are not eligible for bulk purchasing. As part of the eligibility verification process, we may request documentation such as a business license, tax-exempt certificate, or proof of institutional affiliation. This ensures that our bulk order services are being utilized by legitimate organizations that require larger quantities for operational purposes.

The minimum order quantity for a bulk purchase at Acme Shop is typically 100 units per product. However, this requirement may vary depending on the specific product category, seasonal availability, or promotional events. Customers interested in placing a bulk order should check the respective product page for detailed information on minimum quantities. Once you've verified your eligibility and determined your order quantity, you can reach out to our dedicated bulk order team via email or phone. They will guide you through the process, discuss any applicable discounts, and provide an estimated delivery time. Please note that bulk orders may incur additional shipping fees due to the size and weight of the order. Delivery times may also vary based on the availability of the products and your location, typically ranging from 7 to 14 business days.

## Volume Discounts

At Acme Shop, we understand the value of purchasing in bulk, which is why we offer competitive volume discounts to our customers. Our discount tiers are designed to provide significant savings based on the quantity of items ordered. To qualify for volume discounts, customers must order a minimum of 50 units of a single product. The discount percentage increases at specific quantity thresholds: 5% off for orders of 50 to 99 units, 10% off for 100 to 199 units, and 15% off for orders exceeding 200 units. These discounts apply automatically at checkout, ensuring a seamless purchasing experience.

To ensure eligibility for volume discounts, all items must be of the same SKU. Please note that volume discounts cannot be combined with other promotional offers or coupon codes. Additionally, products already marked as on sale are excluded from this program. Processing time for bulk orders is generally 2–3 business days longer than standard orders due to the additional handling required. Customers should account for this timeframe when planning their purchases, especially for time-sensitive projects.

In the case of returns or cancellations, the discount will be recalculated based on the revised order total. If the new order total does not meet the minimum quantity required for a discount, the full amount of the discount will be deducted from the refund. We encourage customers to review their bulk order quantities carefully before finalizing their purchase to ensure they receive the maximum benefit from our volume discount program. For further assistance, feel free to contact our customer service team, who are more than happy to help with any questions or special requests.

## Fulfilment and Lead Times

At Acme Shop, we understand the importance of timely and efficient delivery for bulk orders. Our fulfilment process for bulk purchases is designed to meet the unique needs of our customers, ensuring both reliability and transparency. Once a bulk order is placed, it typically undergoes a processing period of 2–3 business days. During this time, our team carefully reviews the order details, verifies stock availability, and prepares the items for shipment. Orders are then dispatched through our trusted network of logistics partners, with shipping lead times varying based on the destination and shipping method selected. For domestic bulk orders, you can generally expect delivery within 5–7 business days after processing, while international orders may take 10–15 business days.

We strive to keep our customers informed every step of the way. Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number to monitor its progress in real-time. Please note that lead times may be extended during peak seasons or due to unforeseen circumstances such as customs delays or adverse weather conditions. To qualify for bulk order processing, a minimum purchase amount or quantity may be required, which can vary depending on the product category. Additionally, shipping fees for bulk orders are calculated based on the total weight and dimensions of the shipment, as well as the delivery location. Our customer service team is available to assist with any questions or special requirements you may have, ensuring a seamless experience from order placement to delivery.
