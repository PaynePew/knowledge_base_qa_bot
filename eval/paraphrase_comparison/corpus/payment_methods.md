# Payment Methods

## Accepted Cards

Acme Shop accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit cards. We also support PayPal and Apple Pay at checkout. Prepaid gift cards branded with a major network are accepted, but virtual single-use card numbers are declined because they cannot be authorised for the deferred capture our order flow uses.

## Payment Authorization

When an order is placed, we authorise the card for the order total but do not capture funds until the items ship. The authorisation hold is released by the issuing bank within three to five business days if an order is cancelled before shipment. Split shipments capture each parcel separately, so customers may see several smaller charges rather than one combined charge.

## Failed Payments

If a payment is declined, the order is held in a pending state for twenty-four hours so the customer can update the card without losing the cart. After twenty-four hours the pending order is cancelled automatically and any authorisation hold is released. The three most common decline reasons are an incorrect security code, an expired card, and an address that does not match the bank's records.
