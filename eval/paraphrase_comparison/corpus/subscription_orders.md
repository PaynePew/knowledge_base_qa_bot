# Subscription Orders

## Setting Up a Subscription

Eligible consumable products such as filters and batteries can be placed on a recurring subscription at checkout, with delivery intervals of thirty, sixty, or ninety days. Subscription orders receive a standing five percent discount off the regular price, applied automatically to every recurring shipment.

## Managing a Subscription

A subscription can be paused, skipped, or rescheduled from the subscriptions tab in the account, and changes made at least two days before the next scheduled charge take effect immediately. Cancelling a subscription stops all future charges but does not refund a shipment that has already been billed.

## Subscription Pricing Changes

When the regular price of a subscribed product changes, the new price applies to the next shipment after the change, and the member is emailed at least seven days in advance. The standing five percent subscription discount is always calculated against the current regular price.
