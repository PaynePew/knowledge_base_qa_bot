# Account Management

## Password Reset

To reset a forgotten password, choose the "Forgot password" link on the sign-in screen and enter the email address on file. A reset link valid for one hour is sent to that address. Following the link lets the customer choose a new password, which must contain at least ten characters including one number and one symbol.

## Update Email Address

Customers can change the email address tied to their account from the profile settings page. After submitting a new address, a confirmation message is sent to it; the change takes effect only once that confirmation link is followed. The previous address continues to receive order notifications until the new one is verified.

## Close Account

A customer may permanently close an account from the privacy section of profile settings. Closing an account removes saved payment methods and shipping addresses immediately, but order history is retained for seven years to satisfy tax and warranty obligations.
