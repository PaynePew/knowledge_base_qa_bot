# Account Management

## Password Reset

If you've forgotten your Acme Shop account password, don’t worry—resetting it is a straightforward process. First, navigate to the Acme Shop homepage and click on the "Sign In" option located at the top right corner. On the login page, select the "Forgot Password?" link. You will be prompted to enter the email address associated with your Acme Shop account. Once submitted, check your email inbox for a password reset link. This email usually arrives within a few minutes but can take up to 15 minutes during peak times. If you don’t see it, please check your spam or junk folder. Clicking the link will direct you to a secure page where you can enter a new password. For security purposes, ensure your new password is at least eight characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.

If you encounter issues, such as not receiving the reset email, double-check that you entered the correct email address. Only registered users can reset their passwords; if you're unsure which email you used, try searching your inbox for previous Acme Shop communications. Be aware that the password reset link is time-sensitive and will expire after 24 hours, so prompt action is recommended. There is no fee to reset your password, and the process can be repeated if needed. Should you require further assistance, our customer support team is available 24/7 via chat or phone to guide you through the process.

## Update Email Address

To update your email address on your Acme Shop account, start by logging into your account using your current credentials. Once logged in, navigate to the "Account Settings" section, typically found by clicking on your profile icon in the top right corner of the page. From there, select "Personal Information" and locate the "Email Address" field. Click the "Edit" button next to your current email address. You will be prompted to enter your new email address and, for security purposes, your account password. After confirming your new email address, click "Save Changes."

Once you've updated your email address, a confirmation email will be sent to your new address. This email will contain a verification link that you need to click to complete the update process. Be sure to check your spam or junk folder if you do not see the email in your inbox within a few minutes. Typically, the email should arrive within 5-10 minutes. If you do not complete the verification within 24 hours, the change will be automatically canceled for security reasons, and your account will revert to the previous email address.

Please note that updating your email address is free of charge and can be done at any time. However, if you encounter any issues, such as not receiving the verification email or entering an incorrect address, you can contact our customer support team for assistance. Ensure that the new email address is not already associated with another Acme Shop account to avoid complications. If you no longer have access to your old email address and cannot log into your account, you may need to go through additional verification steps by contacting customer support directly.

## Close Account

If you've decided to permanently close your Acme Shop account, we're here to help you through the process. To initiate the closure, log into your account and navigate to the "Account Settings" section. Within this menu, you'll find an option labeled "Close Account." Click on this option and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your request. Please note that once your account is closed, your purchase history and personal information will be permanently deleted, and this action cannot be undone.

Before proceeding, ensure that all outstanding orders are completed and there are no pending refunds or returns, as these cannot be processed after account closure. If you have any remaining gift card balances, use them before closing your account, as they will be forfeited. Account closure is typically completed within 48 hours of your request. During this period, you can contact our support team if you have any questions or change your mind.

Closing an account is free of charge, and there are no hidden fees. However, if you participate in our subscription services or loyalty programs, ensure that these are canceled separately to avoid future charges. If you encounter any issues during the process or have specific circumstances, such as legal concerns or data privacy requests, please reach out to our customer support team for personalized assistance.
