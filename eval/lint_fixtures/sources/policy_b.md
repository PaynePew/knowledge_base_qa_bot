# Refund Policy — Extended Processing (Policy B)

## Refund Timeline

All approved refunds are processed within **14 business days** of receiving the returned item.
During peak season (November–January), processing may take up to 21 business days.
Customers will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been issued.

## Eligibility

Items must be returned within 60 days of purchase. Both new and refurbished items are eligible.

## Contact

For expedited refund processing, contact priority-support@example.com.
