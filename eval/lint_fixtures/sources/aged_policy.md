# Membership Policy — Legacy Version

## VIP Membership Fee

The annual VIP membership fee is $99. Members receive free standard shipping
on all orders, priority customer support, and early access to sales.

## Cancellation

Members may cancel at any time. Refunds are prorated for the remaining months
of the membership term.

## Benefits

- Free standard shipping (no minimum order)
- 10% discount on all purchases
- Dedicated support line
