# About Us — Shipping Overview

## Shipping at ExampleCo

ExampleCo ships to all 50 US states and to over 40 international countries.
Standard shipping takes 5–7 business days. Express shipping (2 business days)
is available for an additional fee.

## Carriers

We partner with UPS, FedEx, and USPS for domestic deliveries. International
orders ship via DHL or FedEx International Priority.

## Tracking

All orders include a tracking number sent via email once the order ships.
Visit our [[order-tracking]] page to check status in real time.
