# Shipping Information

## Delivery Timelines

Standard shipping: 5–7 business days within the contiguous United States.
Express shipping: 2 business days, available at checkout.
International shipping: 10–14 business days depending on destination.

## Shipping Partners

Domestic orders are fulfilled by UPS, FedEx, or USPS. International orders
ship via DHL Express or FedEx International. Carrier selection is based on
destination and package weight.

## Tracking Your Order

A tracking number is emailed automatically once your order ships. You can
also check order status via the [[order-tracking]] portal.
