# Refund Policy — Standard Processing (Policy A)

## Refund Timeline

All approved refunds are processed within **5 business days** of receiving the returned item.
Customers will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been issued to their original
payment method.

## Eligibility

Items must be returned within 30 days of purchase in original condition. Final sale items
are not eligible for refunds.

## Contact

For refund inquiries, contact support@example.com or call our helpline during business hours.
